# ScheduleFinder
## Welcome to our college schedule building application!
### Built with React, Express, MongoDB and Redux using Typescript.
### Our platform offers a streamlined way for students to create their ideal schedule for the week. With just a few clicks, you can easily add your courses for Monday through Friday, including important details such as location and professor name.

## Make meaningful connections with other users.
### But that's not all - we also provide a unique feature that allows users to connect with other students during their free time slots. You can filter through various categories such as age, major, school, gender, and more to find like-minded peers to study with, grab coffee, or simply hang out. With our user-friendly interface and advanced search options, you'll be able to create the perfect schedule and connect with fellow students in no time.

## Easily see when both you and your friend are free.
### Our innovative application takes the hassle out of scheduling by allowing you to effortlessly preview when you and your friends are available on any given day. With just a click of a button, our intuitive design provides a clear and concise view of everyone's free time slots, making it easy to coordinate your schedules.
